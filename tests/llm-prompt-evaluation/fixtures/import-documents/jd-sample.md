# Job Description: Staff Product Manager - AI Platform

## About the Role

We're looking for a Staff Product Manager to lead our AI Platform team. You'll define the strategy and roadmap for our internal AI infrastructure that powers features across all our products. This is a high-impact role reporting directly to the VP of Product.

## Context

Our AI Platform serves 15 product teams and handles 100M+ inference requests daily. We're at an inflection point: demand for AI capabilities is growing 10x faster than our current infrastructure can scale. We need a product leader who can balance platform stability with rapid innovation.

## Responsibilities

### Strategy & Vision
- Define the 3-year vision for AI Platform capabilities
- Identify build vs. buy decisions for AI infrastructure
- Partner with ML Engineering to set technical direction

### Execution
- Own the AI Platform roadmap and prioritization
- Drive cross-functional alignment with product teams
- Define success metrics and track platform health

### Stakeholder Management
- Serve as the "product owner" for platform consumers
- Communicate platform capabilities and roadmap to leadership
- Manage expectations and negotiate priorities

### Team Building
- Mentor junior PMs on the platform team
- Build strong partnerships with engineering and ML teams
- Represent platform needs in company planning

## Requirements

### Must Have
- 7+ years of product management experience
- 3+ years working on platform/infrastructure products
- Experience with ML/AI products or platforms
- Track record of shipping 0-to-1 products
- Strong technical communication skills

### Nice to Have
- Computer science or engineering background
- Experience at a company with large-scale AI systems
- Familiarity with MLOps and model serving
- Previous startup or high-growth company experience

## Compensation

- **Base salary**: $220,000 - $280,000
- **Equity**: 0.08% - 0.15% (4-year vest)
- **Bonus**: 15-20% target
- **Benefits**: Full health coverage, 401(k) with 4% match, unlimited PTO

## Interview Process

1. Recruiter screen (30 min)
2. Hiring manager interview (45 min)
3. Technical deep dive with engineering (60 min)
4. Product sense case study (60 min)
5. Executive interview (45 min)
6. Team fit conversations (2x 30 min)

## Location

San Francisco (Hybrid - 3 days/week in office) or New York (Remote-friendly with quarterly travel)
