# Power Statement: SecureVault Identity Platform

## Product Overview

SecureVault is an enterprise identity and access management platform that helps organizations secure their workforce and customer identities.

## Target Customer

Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs) and IT Security Directors at organizations with 1,000+ employees managing complex identity landscapes across cloud and on-premises systems.

## The Problem

Enterprise security teams face a perfect storm:

1. **Identity sprawl**: Average employee has 191 passwords across work apps
2. **Breach risk**: 80% of breaches involve compromised credentials
3. **Compliance burden**: GDPR, SOC 2, HIPAA require identity governance
4. **Remote work**: Traditional perimeter security no longer works

The cost of inaction is severe: average breach costs $4.5M, takes 280 days to detect, and causes lasting reputation damage.

## The Solution

SecureVault provides unified identity security:

- **Single Sign-On**: One login for all applications (cloud and on-prem)
- **Adaptive MFA**: Risk-based authentication that doesn't slow users down
- **Identity Governance**: Automated access reviews and compliance reporting
- **Threat Detection**: AI-powered identification of compromised accounts
