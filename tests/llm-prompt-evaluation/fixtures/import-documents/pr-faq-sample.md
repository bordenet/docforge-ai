*San Francisco, CA - March 2026*

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What makes FlowBoard different from Jira, Asana, or Monday?**

A: Traditional PM tools are systems of record - you put data in, you get data out. FlowBoard is a system of intelligence. It connects to your existing tools, understands what's actually happening, and proactively helps you manage the project.

**Q: Does FlowBoard replace our existing tools?**

A: No, FlowBoard connects to your existing tools. Your team keeps using GitHub, Slack, and whatever else they love. FlowBoard just makes sense of all that activity.

### AI & Privacy

**Q: How does the AI work?**

A: FlowBoard uses large language models fine-tuned on project management data. It analyzes patterns in task completion, communication sentiment, and historical project data to generate insights.

**Q: Is our data used to train the AI?**

A: No. Your project data is never used for model training. We use a pre-trained model with your data as context, but nothing leaves your account.

### Pricing

**Q: How much does FlowBoard cost?**

A: Starter: $20/user/month. Professional: $40/user/month with advanced analytics. Enterprise: Custom pricing with SSO and dedicated support.

**Q: Is there a free tier?**

A: We offer a 14-day free trial with full functionality. After that, you'll need a paid plan.

### Integration

**Q: What tools do you integrate with?**

A: We have native integrations with GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Asana, Slack, Teams, Figma, Notion, and Linear. Plus a REST API for custom integrations.
